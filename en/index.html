Welcome to the Artists Map!


O Artists Map is an innovative digital space created to bring together artists from all corners of Brazil and the world in one place. Our goal is to be the bridge between creative talents and clients, facilitating connections, promoting collaborations, and expanding job opportunities in the artistic universe.

What do we offer?


On our platform, artists from various fields – such as musicians, actors, dancers, writers, visual artists, circus performers, and others – can create a complete profile that highlights their experiences, qualifications, previous work, and achievements.

Be featured in a true talent catalog. With advanced filters, you can be found to develop projects or participate in events in a simple and practical way.


Why should I join now?


  • Global Visibility: Showcase your talent to the world and reach new audiences.
  • Exclusive Community: A rich exchange of experiences among artists and professionals in the field.
  • Detailed Profiles: Have a personal page with complete information, including portfolio, qualifications, and experiences.
  • Easy Connection: We simplify the contact between artists and clients, creating a safe and reliable environment for partnerships.
  • Diversity and Inclusion: We celebrate cultural richness by connecting talents of different styles, cultures, and regions.
  • Privileged Access: Members-only area, artists' forum, and curated content.
  • Networking: Do business and increase your income by collaborating with other artists to expand the reach of your art!
